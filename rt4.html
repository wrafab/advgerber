BLE Robot

Bluetooth Robot


Assignment-

Your task is to assemble and modify the robot. The robot you assemble will work on building your Arduino skills, robotics and Bluetooth LE skills. You will use an Adafruit Feather BlueFruit LE board that will be controlled by your phone or tablet using the BlueFruit app. You will use this guide to assemble the robot.

1. Assemble the BlueFruit robot.

2. Add a song to the robot.

3. Create at least one piece of armor or one weapon.

4. Take a picture of your robot and describe the rationale for the armor or weapon chosen for your robot.
