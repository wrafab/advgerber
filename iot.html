Internet of Things (IOT)

IOT

The Internet of Things is a extending Internet connectivity to physical devices and ordinary objects. The first IOT device was reportedly in 1982 when a vending machine was hooked up at Carnegie Mellon to determine the inventory. We see IOT devices everyday at work, homes and in our cars. The amount of devices connected to the Internet has grown rapidly in the past few years in areas like farming, manufacturing, retail, home security, transportation and vehicles.
